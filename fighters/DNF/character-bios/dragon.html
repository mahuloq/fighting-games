Speaking of characters with potential, Dragon Knight has tons of it but feels a bit too safe in select ways that prevent her from entering the conversation of being amongst the best of the best. While her adorable dragon companion, Astra, allows her to apply pressure or extend combos in creative and exciting ways, she can struggle against opponents who dominate at a distance or can close the gap with swift and devastating actions.

Still, all things considered, Dragon Knight is far from being a "terrible" character. She just requires a level of focus that can be tough to pin down, as summoning Astra is a crucial part of her playstyle, and it can be challenging to find ways to weave it into your attack strings without messing it up in various ways. In fact, if the other characters left weren't so off the rails, she would likely be in the top five as she was in the beta, but nonetheless...
