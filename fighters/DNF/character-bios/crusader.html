Horrifying in the first beta, horrifying in the second beta, and (you guessed it) horrifying in DNF Duel's full release is Crusader. Summoning a wall, ricocheting you off of it like a pinball with his great hammer, throwing bolts of light at you, you know the drill if you play-tested the betas. While nothing drastic changed with Crusader, he seems a bit "weaker" since the second beta and seems much more manageable when facing him, but that doesn't change much considering he's still easily in the top two.

Crusader's biggest downside, however, is he is the slowest character in the game by far, which means he can -and most likely will- fall off after a short while after everyone figured out how to play against him. But, for now, Crusader remains where he's been since the first beta, as one of the game's best characters.
